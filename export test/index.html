what’s our story?

It is all started with collecting. There is something powerful about it. Can you think of someone who never collected anything - coins from international holidays? Magnets on the fridge? Post cards? Sport cards? Panini stickers? Kinder toys?

Map a Beer Cap is the way to keep track of the beers tasted, the beers-to-be-tasted and breweries visited.

For us it is a thril for hunt and check points collection that drive us to move forward.

We are both curious and always open to try and learn new things.

FAQ

01

What is the weight of the background?
Size S 40X60 is 130 gram, size M 100X140 is 175 gram, size L 200X270 is 240 gram

02

What is the weight of the maps?
Size S (18 caps) is 150 gram, size M (50 caps) is 300 gram, size L (144 caps) is 1 kg.

03

How come that the metalic caps are getting attached to the wooden map?
We use tiny neodim magnets embeded into the map. The weight is so light you cannot even fill it. Magents help you to attach the caps to the exact place. Even if you have a bent cap, you still can easily map it to the right place.

04

How can I fix the map on the wall or stand?
We have a convinient mobile fixing system. Depending on the selected size, you can either attach your map with two hooks on the wall, lean against the cupboard, or use a specially developed standing system.
Take your map with you to the parties, to the new place if you are changing homes, move around your apartment easily.

05

Can I chose a color of a background?
Yes, you can chose the color that better fits into your interior. There are two options at the moment - basalt gray, dark oak and ebony.
